## 📌 Project Overview

This project simulates a real-world Investment Banking M&A engagement at **Bank of America**. As an analyst on the deal team, I supported Managing Director **Susan** and Vice President **Paula** in evaluating strategic M&A opportunities for **Webflix, Inc.**, a leading streaming and entertainment company.

The engagement focused on identifying, analyzing, and pitching **Nintendo Co., Ltd.** as a transformative acquisition target to expand Webflix’s total addressable market (TAM) into interactive entertainment and gaming while deepening its presence in the Asia-Pacific region.

## 🔍 Detailed Task Breakdown

### Task 1: SWOT Analysis – Threats Section
**Objective:** Identify external threats Webflix faces that could impact a potential M&A strategy.

**Key Threats Identified:**
- **Intense Competition & Piracy**: The streaming industry remains highly competitive with Netflix, Disney+, Amazon Prime, and emerging platforms. Content piracy continues to erode revenue.
- **Regulatory & Antitrust Risks**: Increasing scrutiny on big tech and media consolidation (especially in gaming/streaming) could complicate large acquisitions and content licensing deals.

**Deliverable:** Professionally written threats section matching banking/consulting standards.

---

### Task 2: Financial Summary Excel
**Objective:** Build a clean, banker-style financial summary using Webflix’s 10-K.

**Completed Sections:**
- Income Statement (Revenue, Cost of Revenues, Gross Profit, Operating Income)
- Balance Sheet (Cash, Debt, Equity)
- Cash Flow Statement (CFO, CFI, FCF)
- Derived Metrics: Gross Margin, EBITDA, EBITDA Margin, Leverage Ratios (Debt/EBITDA, Debt/Assets, Debt/Book Cap)

**Key Insight:** Webflix showed strong revenue growth but faced margin pressure and negative free cash flow in recent years — highlighting the strategic need for diversification into higher-margin gaming content.

---

### Task 3: M&A Target Screening & Recommendation
**Objective:** Evaluate four potential acquisition targets against Webflix’s stated goals:
- Expand TAM beyond linear/streaming content
- Increase geographic and product diversity
- Avoid music business exposure

**Final Recommendation: Nintendo Co., Ltd.**

**Rationale:**
- Strong IP portfolio (Mario, Pokémon, Zelda)
- Hardware + software ecosystem with high switching costs
- Significant APAC exposure (Japan + Asia growth)
- Highly accretive to Webflix’s valuation and strategic positioning

---

### Task 4: Nintendo DCF Valuation
**Objective:** Build a rigorous, client-ready DCF model to determine Nintendo’s implied enterprise and equity value.

**Model Highlights:**
- 5-year explicit forecast (2022E–2026E)
- Revenue CAGR: 5%
- EBITDA margin expansion: 39.7% → 42.0%
- WACC: 8.5%
- Terminal Multiple: 12.0x
- **Implied Share Price: $78.71**
- **Implied TEV: $69.56B**

**Sensitivity Analysis:** Full two-way table showing valuation range of **$62B – $82B** depending on WACC and exit multiple.

**Deliverable:** Polished Excel file with assumptions, projections, valuation bridge, and sensitivity tables.

---
